## Recommended Free Image Sources

### 1. **Wikimedia Commons** (Best for historical accuracy)
- URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org
- Search: "[Laptop Model] [Year]" (e.g., "Compaq LTE 1994")
- Most images are CC-licensed (free to use with attribution)
- Filter by license in search

### 2. **Archive.org - Vintage Computer Museum**
- URL: https://archive.org
- Search: "[Brand] [Model]"
- Historical computing collections with clear licensing

### 3. **Flickr Commons** 
- URL: https://www.flickr.com/commons
- Search: "retro laptop [model]" or "[brand] vintage"
- Many public domain and CC-licensed historical tech photos

### 4. **Computer Museum Collections**
- Smithsonian Computer History: https://www.si.edu
- Strong collections of classic laptops
- Photos typically public domain

### 5. **Official Manufacturer Resources**
- IBM/Lenovo heritage archive
- Toshiba archives
- HP/Compaq historical photos
- Often high-quality and clearly licensed

## Image Requirements

- **Format:** PNG or JPG (site supports both)
- **Size:** ~400-500px width recommended for consistency
- **Resolution:** 72-96 DPI is fine (web standard)
- **Orientation:** Portrait or landscape (site auto-fits)
- **License:** Must be non-commercial use allowed (CC-BY-SA, CC-BY, public domain)

---

## How to Replace Images

1. **Find and download** a suitable image for each laptop model
2. **Rename** the image to match the filename (e.g., `laptop_macbook.png`)
3. **Place** in `assets/images/` folder
4. **Refresh** your browser to see changes
